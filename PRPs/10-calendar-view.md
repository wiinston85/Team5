# PRP 10: Calendar View

## Feature Overview
Display todos and Singapore holidays on a monthly calendar interface.

## Technical Requirements
- Holidays table and seeded data.
- API: GET /api/holidays.
- Route: /calendar.
- Calendar grid generation for month with leading/trailing days.

## UI Requirements
- Prev, next, and today navigation.
- Highlight weekends and current day.
- Show holiday labels and todo counts.
- Modal listing todos for selected day.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Calendar renders correctly with month navigation.
- Todos and holidays appear on correct dates.

## Testing Requirements
- E2E month navigation, date modal, and holiday display.
